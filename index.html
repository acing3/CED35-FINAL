35 Years of CED
The Council of Entreprenurial Development has been pushing entrepeneurs to soar and create innovations in a time where all seems stagnant. Utilizing resource acquisition as well as time management, CED has bee informing entrpeneurs on the best possible course of action for business longevity as well as profitability. Through access to capital, entreprenur mentorship, as well as signature conferences, CED has managed to boost growth in various business startups, from textiles to tech.
With its driving focus being positive growth for businesses and startups, the CED has managed to influence and benefit over 400 companies globally, informing each of the importance of capital management and the correct course to take when thinking of long term development. The Council for Entreprenurial Development uses informative talks from key individuals to inform business owners of the most effective means of continued expansion.

History of the RTP
Research Triangle Park is one of the largest research parks in the United States, and continues its development despite that title. Located in the heart of North Carolina, the RTP is the focal point for scientific and technological development on the east coast. The Universities connection and coroperation are essentially the foundation for the Triangle. Bolstered by three universities comprising the park's borders, the RTP is central to upcoming development as well as past innovation due to its:
  • Confined by NC State, UNC Chapel Hill, and Duke University
  • Research environments and Proficient networking
  • Business Growth and Entrepeneurship Policies / Opportunities
About
With the intent of documenting the development of the RTP since 1984, UNC Chapel has taken to interviewing and collecting data about various businesses in the Research Triangle. This site is the collection of this information with the purpose of showing how the RTP has developed over the past 35 years, as well as showing the influence that the Council for Entreprenurial Development has had in the area. Each project consists of a plethora of relevant data about a company in the RTP. With information such as business methods as well as production management; each unique business is thoroughly interviewed and documented to provide the most relevant information regarding their development and continued growth plans for the future.
